Lins Food Haus Catering Services is located near the newest shop & business district makati circuit, Catering Different Events with a focus on Excellent Service and Good Food.

Lins Food Haus and Catering Service started off with a prayer, Owned by Aileen Sanchez, together with the Lord’s guidance, and with the help of her friend, who studied Culinary Arts, they have established the business way back 2007. Back then, they rented the utensils and equipment they needed in the business, with no place of their own. After few years of hard work of savings, they had their own equipment and a small place used for preparation. Now, they have a bigger venue used for the events they cater, located at Leiva St. cor Havana St., Sta. Ana, Manila. They cater different events and occasions such as debut party, wedding receptions and even concert events. Mission: To provide excellent service and to serve quality food done in perfection. Vision: The key to success is to meet and exceed the customer's needs in terms of quality of food and excellence of service. We cater for all occasion.
